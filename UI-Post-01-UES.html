Usability Errors Solutions Examples

6pm.com, landing page

In the marked area of the picture, there's a function that gives the user the ability to Log In or Register, or check their account. However, it happens that both "Log In or Register" and "My Account" lead you to the Log In/ Register page if you're not logged in.

I think that this illusion of choice can lead the user to slightly waste their time, by taking an unnecessary step to achieve what the user wants to: check their accounts.

I'd reduce this interaction to the "Login or Register" option, and then have it automatically take you to the "My Account" page, as the end-goal of logging in or registering in a site is making use of the features offered to those that have an account.

NYC Parks, landing page

The whole page is fitted in about the 60% of the horizontal screen size available, causing the content of the web site to feel unnecesarily cluttered and saturated respect to space. This can be countered by distributing the content around the whole area of the screen.
